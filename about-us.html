ABOUT US

Eenie Eats is a home-based catering company of one. Ynhi is a self-taught cook, learning from her grandmother since she was young. Her passion for cooking stems from her family and the delights she sees when they eat her meals. Ynhi specializes in Asian cuisine and the occasional clam chowder.

The business started one afternoon when she helped cater for a family event. One event led to another and by word of mouth she had unwittingly started a small business. From then on she had been cooking on and off, gaining experience through every event. As of 2019, she has been looking to officiate and expand her small business in hopes of expanding her food to others.

Heart, love, and soul are in every bite and she hopes to share this kind of peace with the world.
